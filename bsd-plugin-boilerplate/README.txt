== Installation ==

1. Upload 'bsd-split-pay-stripe-connect-woo.zip' through the WordPress plugins menu.
2. Click **Activate**.
3. Navigate to **WooCommerce** > **Settings** > **Stripe Connect Split Pay** to configure options.
4. Check out our [documentation](https://www.battlestardigital.com/docs/bsd-split-pay-stripe-connect-woo/) for additional information.
